As a school librarian, I’m always exploring tools and technology that can enhance our library services, support student learning, and help teachers collaborate effectively. Sharing these resources here allows everyone to benefit from tools I’ve found valuable in my own practice.

Here are some useful technology tools and resources that can support school librarians in their work:

  • Library Management Systems Tools like Library World help organize and track library collections.
  • Digital Catalogs & Discovery Tools Platforms such as LibGuides support easy discovery of resources for students and teachers.
  • Reading & Literacy Apps Apps like Abdo Digital Bookshelf, TumbleBook Library, and Storyline Online promote student engagement with reading.
  • Content Creation Tools Tools such as Canva and Adobe Express empower librarians and students to create engaging presentations and projects.
  • Collaboration Platforms Google Workspace for Education (Google Docs, Slides, Classroom) makes it easy to co-create and share resources across your school community.
